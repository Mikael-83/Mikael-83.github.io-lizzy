Lizzy Dance Classes

Experience the joy of movement with our comprehensive dance programs! We offer classes for all ages and skill levels, from tiny dancers to advanced performers.

Our dance styles include Ballet, Jazz, Contemporary, Hip Hop, and Tap. Each class is designed to build confidence, coordination, and creativity in a supportive environment.

Lizzy Gymnastics

Build strength, flexibility, and confidence with our gymnastics program! Our certified coaches provide expert instruction in a safe, fun environment.

We offer recreational and competitive gymnastics programs, including floor exercise, vault, bars, and beam. Perfect for developing coordination and discipline.

Lizzy Fast Track

Our intensive Fast Track program is designed for dedicated students ready to accelerate their skills. This elite training program combines dance and gymnastics fundamentals with advanced techniques.

Fast Track students receive personalized coaching, performance opportunities, and preparation for competitions and showcases.
